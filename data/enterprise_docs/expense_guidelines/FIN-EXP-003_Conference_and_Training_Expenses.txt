NOVATECH ENTERPRISES
FINANCIAL POLICY — FIN-EXP-003
CONFERENCE AND TRAINING EXPENSE GUIDELINES
Effective Date: 1 January 2026 | Version: 2.0

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. PURPOSE
NovaTech invests in employee professional development. This policy outlines how
conference attendance, external training, and professional development costs are
approved and reimbursed.

2. ANNUAL TRAINING BUDGET
Each employee has an annual professional development budget:
  - Individual Contributors:   USD 2,000 per year
  - Senior / Lead employees:   USD 3,000 per year
  - Managers:                  USD 4,000 per year
  - Directors and above:       Approved case-by-case by CHRO

Budget resets on 1 January. Unused budget does not carry over to the next year.

3. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
The following are eligible within the annual budget:
  - Conference registration fees (including virtual conferences)
  - Online course platforms (Coursera, Pluralsight, LinkedIn Learning, Udemy for Business)
  - Professional certification exam fees and preparation materials
  - Technical books and study guides (up to USD 200 per year)
  - Workshop and bootcamp fees

4. APPROVAL PROCESS
All training expenses above USD 500 require manager approval before purchase.
Expenses above USD 1,500 require both manager and department head approval.
Conference travel expenses (flights, hotel) are covered by the Travel Expense Policy
(FIN-EXP-001) and do NOT count toward the training budget.

5. CONFERENCES
Employees may attend up to 2 external conferences per year within their budget.
For conferences above USD 1,000 registration fee, the employee must agree to share
learnings with their team via a written summary or presentation within 30 days
of return.

6. CERTIFICATION REIMBURSEMENT BONUS
Upon passing an approved professional certification exam, NovaTech provides a
one-time bonus above the training budget:
  - Foundational certifications (e.g., AZ-900, AWS Cloud Practitioner):  USD 200
  - Associate-level certifications (e.g., AZ-104, AWS Solutions Architect):  USD 500
  - Expert/Professional certifications (e.g., AZ-305, CISSP):  USD 1,000

Bonus is paid via payroll in the month after proof of certification is submitted to HR.

7. REPAYMENT
If an employee leaves NovaTech within 12 months of completing training costing over
USD 1,000, a pro-rated repayment may be required:
  - Depart within 6 months: 100% repayment
  - Depart between 6–12 months: 50% repayment
  - Depart after 12 months: No repayment

This repayment obligation must be acknowledged in writing before approval of training
costing over USD 1,000.

8. SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES
Team-level subscriptions (e.g., Pluralsight team plan) are purchased centrally by IT
and do not count against individual budgets. Employees should check with IT before
purchasing an individual subscription that may already be covered.
