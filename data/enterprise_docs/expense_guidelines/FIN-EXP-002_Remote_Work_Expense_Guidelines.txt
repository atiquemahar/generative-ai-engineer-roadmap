NOVATECH ENTERPRISES
FINANCIAL POLICY — FIN-EXP-002
REMOTE WORK EQUIPMENT AND EXPENSE GUIDELINES
Effective Date: 1 March 2026 | Version: 1.4

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. OVERVIEW
NovaTech supports a hybrid work model. This policy defines what equipment and home
office expenses are reimbursable for employees working remotely for 3 or more days
per week under an approved remote work arrangement.

2. ELIGIBILITY
Employees must have a signed Remote Work Agreement on file with HR before submitting
remote work expenses. Temporary remote work arrangements (fewer than 30 consecutive
days) are not eligible for equipment reimbursement.

3. INITIAL SETUP ALLOWANCE
Eligible remote workers receive a one-time home office setup allowance of USD 800.
This covers any combination of the following:
  - External monitor(s)
  - Ergonomic keyboard and mouse
  - Desk lamp
  - Webcam (if not integrated into laptop)
  - Headset for calls
  - Desk or office chair (up to USD 400 of the total allowance)

All items must be purchased within 90 days of approval and submitted via Concur.
Receipts required. Items remain property of NovaTech and must be returned upon
departure.

4. INTERNET CONNECTIVITY
A monthly internet stipend of USD 50 is provided to all approved remote workers.
This is processed as a non-taxable reimbursement via payroll and does not require
a monthly receipt submission. Employees on hybrid arrangements (fewer than 3 days
remote per week) are not eligible for this stipend.

5. ONGOING CONSUMABLES
The following recurring items are reimbursable up to USD 20 per month with receipt:
  - Printer paper and ink (where printing is a documented job requirement)
  - USB cables and minor accessories

Software subscriptions are NOT reimbursable as personal purchases; all software
must be requisitioned through IT via IT-PROC-002.

6. ANNUAL REFRESH
Employees may request equipment replacement after 3 years of use or in the event of
equipment failure. Replacement is subject to manager and Finance approval. Failed
equipment must be reported to IT who will coordinate repair or replacement.

7. COWORKING SPACES
NovaTech maintains memberships at WeWork locations in 12 cities. Employees may use
these at no personal cost by booking via the intranet. Ad-hoc coworking space costs
are reimbursable up to USD 40 per day with a maximum of 8 days per month, subject
to manager approval.

8. EXCLUSIONS
The following are not reimbursable: home office rent or mortgage contribution, utility
bills (electricity, heating), personal mobile phone plans, standing desks (covered
by the setup allowance if within budget), and any item purchased before remote work
agreement approval.
