# NovaTech Enterprises
## HR-LEA-003 | Parental Leave Policy
**Effective Date:** 1 April 2026 | **Version:** 3.0 | **Policy Owner:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Policy Statement

NovaTech Enterprises is committed to supporting employees through the transition to parenthood.
This policy provides enhanced parental leave entitlements that exceed statutory minimums in all
jurisdictions where we operate.

## 2. Eligibility

This policy applies to all employees who:

- Have been continuously employed for **at least 6 months** before the expected week of
  childbirth or placement for adoption, AND
- Give the required notice as detailed in Section 4

Employees on fixed-term contracts are eligible if their contract is expected to extend through
the leave period.

## 3. Leave Entitlements

### Primary Caregiver Leave (Birth or Adoption)

| Period | Pay |
|---|---|
| Weeks 1–16 | 100% of base salary |
| Weeks 17–26 | 60% of base salary |
| Weeks 27–52 | Statutory pay only |

### Secondary Caregiver / Partner Leave

| Period | Pay |
|---|---|
| Weeks 1–4 | 100% of base salary |

Secondary caregiver leave may be taken any time within the first 12 months after birth or
placement. It may be taken in blocks of 1 week minimum.

### Adoption Leave

Adoption leave mirrors birth leave provisions. Employees adopting a child under 18 are
entitled to the same leave as the primary caregiver. Prospective adoptive parents may also
take up to 5 days paid leave to attend adoption appointments.

## 4. Notification Requirements

Employees expecting a child must notify HR and their manager:

- **Birth:** No later than the 15th week before the expected week of childbirth
- **Adoption:** Within 7 days of being matched with a child

Notification must be made via the Parental Leave form on Workday and include the expected
start date of leave and intended duration.

## 5. Keeping in Touch Days

Employees on parental leave may work up to **10 Keeping in Touch (KIT) days** without
ending their leave entitlement. KIT days are paid at normal daily rate in addition to
any parental pay received. Agreement from the line manager is required.

## 6. Return to Work

Employees returning from parental leave have the right to return to the same role. If that
role is no longer available due to a genuine redundancy situation, they must be offered a
suitable alternative. Requests to return on a flexible or part-time basis will be considered
under the Flexible Working Policy (HR-FLX-001).

## 7. Additional Support

- **Fertility Treatment Leave:** Up to 5 days paid leave per cycle of IVF or equivalent treatment
- **Pregnancy Loss:** Up to 10 days paid leave following pregnancy loss at any stage
- **Neonatal Care:** Additional paid leave matching any period a newborn spends in neonatal care
