# NovaTech Enterprises
## HR-LEA-002 | Sick Leave Policy
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026 | **Version:** 2.3 | **Policy Owner:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy ensures that employees can take necessary time off when they are ill without
financial hardship, while establishing fair and transparent procedures to manage absence.

## 2. Sick Leave Entitlement

| Length of Service | Paid Sick Leave Per Year |
|---|---|
| 0–1 year | 10 days at full pay |
| 1–3 years | 15 days at full pay |
| 3+ years | 20 days at full pay |

After entitlement is exhausted, extended sick leave is covered at 60% of base salary for
a further 90 days, subject to medical certification. Thereafter, long-term disability
provisions under the company insurance plan may apply.

## 3. Reporting Absence

Employees must notify their line manager or HR Business Partner **before the start of their
working day** or as soon as reasonably practicable if they are unable to attend work due to
illness. Notification must be given by phone or approved messaging platform — email alone
is not sufficient for same-day absence.

For absences of **more than 3 consecutive days**, a medical certificate from a registered
physician must be provided to HR within 5 days of returning to work.

## 4. Return to Work

Following any absence of **5 or more consecutive days**, the employee must complete a
Return to Work meeting with their manager within 2 days of returning. The purpose is to:

- Confirm fitness for work
- Identify any workplace adjustments needed
- Provide an opportunity for the employee to share any concerns

This meeting is supportive, not disciplinary.

## 5. Absence Trigger Points

HR will initiate a formal absence review if any of the following occur:

- **8 or more separate absences** in a rolling 12-month period
- **20 or more calendar days** of sickness absence in a rolling 12-month period
- A pattern of absence (e.g., recurring absences on Mondays or Fridays)

Reviews are conducted by HR in conjunction with the line manager and are intended to
provide support, not punishment.

## 6. Long-Term Sickness

For absences exceeding 28 calendar days, HR will conduct regular check-ins (at least monthly)
with the absent employee. An Occupational Health referral will be made at 28 days or sooner
if appropriate. NovaTech will make reasonable adjustments to support a return to work.

## 7. Mental Health Absences

NovaTech treats mental health conditions the same as physical health conditions. Employees
are encouraged to be open about mental health-related absences but are not required to
disclose a specific diagnosis. The Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) provides free and
confidential counselling — call 0800 555 1234 or visit the EAP portal.
