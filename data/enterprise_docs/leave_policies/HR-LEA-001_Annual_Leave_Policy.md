# NovaTech Enterprises
## HR-LEA-001 | Annual Leave Policy
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026 | **Version:** 4.1 | **Policy Owner:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Entitlement

All permanent full-time employees are entitled to paid annual leave as follows:

| Years of Service | Annual Leave Entitlement |
|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 20 days per year |
| 2–5 years | 23 days per year |
| 5–10 years | 25 days per year |
| 10+ years | 28 days per year |

Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis. Contractors and fixed-term employees
receive the minimum statutory entitlement as per their local jurisdiction.

## 2. Leave Year

The leave year runs from **1 January to 31 December**. Leave is accrued on a monthly basis.
New employees begin accruing leave from their first day of employment.

## 3. Booking Leave

All annual leave must be requested and approved via the HR self-service portal (Workday) with
a minimum of:

- **2 weeks notice** for leave of 1–5 days
- **4 weeks notice** for leave of 6–10 days
- **8 weeks notice** for leave exceeding 10 consecutive days

Approval is at the manager's discretion, taking into account business requirements and team
coverage. Managers must respond to leave requests within 5 business days.

## 4. Carry-Over

A maximum of **5 days** of annual leave may be carried over into the following leave year.
Carried-over leave must be taken by **31 March** of the following year or it is forfeited.

Exceptions to carry-over limits may be granted by HR where leave could not be taken due to
documented business necessity. Requests must be submitted by 15 December.

## 5. Leave During Notice Period

Employees serving a notice period may take any accrued but untaken leave during the notice
period subject to manager agreement. NovaTech reserves the right to require employees to take
outstanding leave during their notice period.

## 6. Public Holidays

Annual leave entitlement is in addition to the public holidays applicable in each employee's
jurisdiction. A list of public holidays by location is maintained on the HR intranet and
updated annually by 1 December.

## 7. Pay During Leave

Annual leave is paid at the employee's normal base salary. Variable pay elements (commission,
overtime, bonuses) are not included in leave pay calculations unless required by local law.

## 8. Excessive Leave Balances

Employees holding more than 1.5x their annual entitlement in accrued leave will be notified
by HR and required to reduce their balance. Managers may direct employees to take leave to
bring balances within acceptable limits.
